Privacy Policy

White Tiger Marketing LLC — Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Overview

White Tiger Marketing LLC ("White Tiger Marketing," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website white-tiger-marketing.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or book a strategy call with us.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

When you book a strategy call through our site, you submit information through our scheduling tool. This may include your name, email address, and information about your business. We use this solely to facilitate the call and follow up as appropriate.

b. Automatically Collected Information

We use Google Analytics to collect data about how visitors interact with our website. This includes pages visited, time spent on the site, device type, general geographic location, and referral source. This data is aggregated and used to understand how our site performs and to improve the user experience.

We also use Google Tag Manager to manage tracking scripts on the site.

c. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and the third-party tools we use (including Google Analytics and our scheduling embed) may place cookies or similar tracking technologies on your device. These help us understand site usage and enable core functionality such as scheduling.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to strategy call bookings and conduct consultations
  • Understand how visitors use our website and improve it accordingly
  • Track which marketing channels and pages are performing
  • Communicate with prospective and current clients

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services that may collect or process data on our behalf:

  • Google Analytics — for website traffic and behavior analysis. Google's privacy policy governs data collected through this service.
  • Scheduling platform — used to facilitate strategy call bookings. The scheduling provider's own terms and privacy policy apply to data submitted through their interface.

These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

5. Data Retention

We retain information submitted through our scheduling tool for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as required to maintain a business relationship. Analytics data is retained in accordance with Google Analytics' standard retention settings.

6. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete personal information you have provided to us by contacting us directly. We will respond to such requests in a reasonable timeframe.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are directed at business owners and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available on our website with the effective date noted at the top.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, email us at contact@white-tiger-marketing.com.
